This card accepts a source of events, such as _click events_ on the `‚ñ¨ toggle` interactive piece. As a result, it creates **a sequence of events over time**. This sequence is called a **stream**.

Reactive programming is programming with **streams**. With the full deck of cards, you'll be able to:

- **create** a stream from various sources
- **transform** and **filter** a stream in different ways
- **combine** multiple streams
- and more!
